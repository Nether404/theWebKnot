Yes! We offer a **14-day free trial** of Premium features. No credit card required.

### What happens if I cancel?

You keep Premium access until the end of your billing period. After that, you revert to the free tier. All your data is retained for 30 days.

### Can I switch between monthly and annual?

Yes! You can switch anytime. If switching from monthly to annual, you'll receive a prorated credit.

### Do I need Premium to use LovaBolt?

No! LovaBolt works great on the free tier. Premium is for power users who want unlimited AI and advanced features.

### Can I upgrade/downgrade my plan?

Yes! Upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes take effect immediately with prorated billing.

### Is there a student discount?

Yes! Students get **50% off** with valid student email. Contact hello@lovabolt.com with proof of enrollment.

### Is there a non-profit discount?

Yes! Non-profits get **50% off**. Contact hello@lovabolt.com with proof of non-profit status.

### Can I use my own Gemini API key?

Not currently. Custom API key support is planned for Enterprise plan.

You can add more team members for $5/month per additional member, or upgrade to Enterprise.

### Is my payment information secure?

Yes! All payments are processed by Stripe, a PCI DSS Level 1 certified payment processor. We never store your card details.
